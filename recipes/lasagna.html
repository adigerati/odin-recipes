Lasagna

Lasagna Garfield Style

This lasagna is the cat's meow. No toothpicks needed, the cathair should take care of that.

Ingredients

  • One oversized cat.
  • One oversized lasagna.
  • A pinch of a salty chef.

Steps

  1. Pet the cat.
  2. Cook the lasagna.
  3. Give the cat the lasagna.
  4. Once the cat passes out from a food coma, insert cat into lasagna.
  5. Enjoy!
